This is a book based on lecture notes I took during Differential and Integral Calculus I and II courses as a student of Bioprocess and Biotechnology Engineering at the School of Agricultural Sciences of the São Paulo State University.

There are multiple volumes covering various topics from Calculus while presenting examples, problems and solving problems strategies, which is directly linked with the main goal of the book: bring high quality materials that are accessible for undegrad students
and help them with their studies.
